About Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental disorder that affects communication, behavior, and social interactions. Individuals with ASD may have unique strengths and challenges, and they often experience sensory sensitivities. Early diagnosis and support can greatly improve the quality of life for individuals with ASD.

Symptoms of ASD vary widely but often include difficulties with social interactions, repetitive behaviors, and specific interests. With a spectrum of traits and characteristics, ASD is unique to each individual, making personalized support essential.
